The White Lotus

Short Summary

Season 1: Set in a tropical resort, the series explores the lives of guests and staff over a week, revealing dark secrets and social dynamics.

Season 2: The story shifts to a new location, delving into the complexities of relationships and the impact of wealth.

Season 3: The narrative continues to unravel the intricacies of human behavior against the backdrop of luxury and privilege.

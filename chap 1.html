How to Make Filipino Style Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 lb spaghetti noodles
  • 1/2 lb shredded baked chicken breast
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoon grated lemon peel

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. After that set it aside
  2. In a large bowl,combine the egg yolk,heavy cream,parmesan cheese,basil, and parsley. Whisk and set aside.
  3. Heat the oil in a wide cooking pot or casserole
  4. Put in the garlic and cook until it turns light brown
  5. Add-in the shredded baked chicken. Stir. Cook for 1 minute
  6. Put in the cooked spaghetti noodles. After that toss the noodles with the chicken
  7. Adjust the heat to low and add the heavy cream and egg mixture. Toss it until all of the ingredients are well distributed.
  8. Season it with salt and pepper
  9. Transfer it to a serving plate. Garnish it with some greated lemon peel on top
